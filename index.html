Slime Wants to Exp+3!

This is a pixel - style casual game for two players. Inspired by the game "Gold Miner", it adds some new mechanisms, such as skills, interruptions, resource snatching, and vertical movement, turning it into a rather competitive game. Two slimes can either collect or snatch each other's water droplets, and they can also cut each other's tentacles to hinder the opponent. It's a casual two - player game that combines both cooperation and competition, which can enhance the friendship between players.

Programmer, Game Designer

October 2023

Completed G-Bits 48H University Game Jam Challenge

2D Pixel - style Two - player Puzzle Strategy Game

Technologies used: C#, Unity

Project Management: Managed the project

Game Design: Designed game gameplay, combat system.

Programming: Implemented resource collection and snatching function.

Game Background: There is a slime named Blue. Before coming to this cave, it was too weak and could only crawl furtively. But one day, Blue found that there was magical water in this strange cave. After absorbing the water here, its EXP started to grow crazily! At this time, another slime named Green came to drink the water from the same source. After they both finished absorbing the water in the puddle, they looked up and found that magical water droplets were falling from the top of the cave! In order to become the level - 100 slime king as soon as possible, they will use their excellent crawling skills and very useful tentacles to absorb all the water here.

Core gameplay: Both players (slimes) need to use their tentacles to suck up water droplets. It takes time to suck up items, and the heavier the water droplet is, the longer it takes to suck it up. Once a water droplet is sucked onto the slime, it can devour the droplet and start digesting and absorbing it. The more water there is to be digested, the higher the absorption efficiency will be. By absorbing the magic in the water, the slimes can gain experience points. When they have gained enough experience points, they can improve their abilities and choose one of the following three options: the maximum number of tentacles, the tentacle dragging force, or a random special skill. During the sucking process, if a tentacle is attacked by the opponent's tentacle, the player's tentacle will be broken, and they will be stunned, unable to move or launch tentacles for a certain period of time. At the same time, the attacked tentacle will spit out some of the incompletely absorbed water, which will be added to the dragged water droplet. After the stun ends, the broken tentacle will be repaired and can be used again.

Air Command

This is a game that combines the gameplay of Flight Chess and card - based battles. Players have four cards in hand, each with different functions and points. During their turn, players can choose to use the function of a hand - held card or use the points on the card to move their fighter jets.

Programmer, Game Designer

March 2023 - April 2023

Technologies used: C++, SFML

Game Design: Designed and implemented a board game combining Flight Chess and cards

Programming: Implemented Flight Chess functions and various card - based combat functions.

When a fighter jet reaches a designated position, the player can increase their reputation points. When a player's fighter jet meets an opponent's fighter jet, a battle begins. The battle is divided into an offensive side and a defensive side. Within a specified time, players choose one of their hand - held cards as the battle points and another as a tactical function to engage in combat with the opponent. The winner will plunder some of the loser's reputation points. After reaching the maximum number of rounds, the player with more reputation points wins.
